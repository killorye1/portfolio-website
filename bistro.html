Main Street Bistro

Case Study


Main Street Bistro is located at 59 Main Street in the heart of downtown, New Paltz, New York. They serve breakfast and lunch in a comfortable, casual atmosphere. They specialize in eclectic American and Vegetarian cuisine, but their varied menu features a wide range of dishes for every taste. The goal of this project was to redesign their website to create a digital presence that is consistent with the character of the physical restaurant.
